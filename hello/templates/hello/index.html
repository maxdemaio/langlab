About

LangLab is a free and open source website where you can practice the conjugations of the 25 most common verbs in a target language. This allows for practical and efficient language learning, giving you a well founded basis to immediately comprehend and converse. With the use of frequency analysis, your practice will be direct, studying the most useful pillars of a foreign language.

In addition, helpful resources have been provided to supplement your learning process. Finding content you enjoy serves as an amazing springboard to learning a foreign language. These resources will provide a great start to any language learner's journey.
